Title: Cat & Bear Series

Year: 2022

Medium: Digital, yarns, mixed media

Description: This is an endearing IP character design venture, introducing the charming Matcha Bear and Red Bean Cat. Evolving from concept to tangible expressions, they inhabit WeChat stickers that garnered over 150 downloads. Extending their reach, I transmuted them into plush pillows, carpets, and acrylic stands, fostering their presence in diverse mediums. Moreover, their image was woven into a tufting project, transcending virtual boundaries. This body of work not only showcases artistic versatility but kindles an endearing connection between the characters and a growing audience, epitomizing the symbiotic relationship between design and interaction.
