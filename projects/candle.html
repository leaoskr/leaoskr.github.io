Title: Repetation: Candels

Year: 2022

Size: 5”x6.5”x5.75” (left); 4.25”x4.25”x15” (middle); 3.5”x6.5”x11.5” (right)

Medium: Wood, steel flat bars

Description: This project is an exploration of uniformity through diversity. I reshaped identical elements into distinct forms, each a unique iteration of the same item. Steel flat bars are employed to craft fluid, dynamic flames—an intriguing contrast to their rigid composition. In three variations—low, medium, and high—the project delves into the interplay between consistency and change, offering a fresh perspective on the malleability of materials and the intrigue of repetition in art.
