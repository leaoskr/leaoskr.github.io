Title: A Beginner’s Guide To KiKi Watching

Team: Producer: Nellie Tonev | Programmer: Nellie Tonev, Lea Shi, Courtney Singleton | Artist: Ye Wei, Jose Mireles, Lea Shi | Sound Designer: Courtney Singleton

Year: 2024

Medium: Unity, Meta Quest 3

Description: Step into a beautiful VR world in "A Beginner’s Guide to KiKi Watching", where your role as a butterfly watcher is simple: explore the natural environment and capture photos of its most interesting creature, KiKi, butterflies with triangular wings. The major challenge of this game is to give the player a high degree of agency within the world. Designed for Meta Quest 3, this immersive experience invites you to roam freely through a stunning nature, all without a tutorial or guidance. As a first-time player, you’ll discover how to interact with this world at your own pace, learning the ropes of photography while uncovering secrets hidden in the environment. Are you ready to find KiKis and photograph the wonders of nature?
