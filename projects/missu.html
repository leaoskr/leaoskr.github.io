Title: Miss U

Year: 2018

Size: 6”x22”x13”

Medium: Clay, paper, wood, and felt

Description: Inspired by my favorite book, The Little Prince, written by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, I created this artwork to show two important stories in The Little Prince. On the left side, it shows when the Little Prince meeting his best friend, the Fox, on Earth. On the right side, it shows the Little Prince walking on his own planet, B612, with the Rose. I wanted to depict the main theme of the book, which is friendship and love, into the three-dimensional art piece.
