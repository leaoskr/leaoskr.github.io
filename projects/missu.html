Title: Miss U

Year: 2022

Size: 1920 Pixels x 1080 Pixels

Medium: Digital

Description: Crafted through the synergy of Photoshop and Adobe After Effects, my 2D animation resonates with personal sentiment. It narrates a tale of yearning for home, wherein a girl encounters her parents in a dream, ignited by the passage of a magical shooting star. Beyond designing the central characters, I envisaged my family in animal forms, drawing from my intimate understanding of each member. This creation serves as an affectionate tribute, a manifestation of love for my family. More than an artistic endeavor, it's a profound reflection on emotions, a visual expression of connection, and an encapsulation of cherished relationships that transcend the realm of dreams and artistry.

This work was accepted into the first UMass Animated Film Festival.
