Greetings! I'm thrilled to welcome you to my portfolio website. I am currently pursuing a double major in B.S. Computer Science and B.A. Studio Art at UMass Amherst, where I'm cultivating my passion for both the digital realm and the world of artistic expression.

With an insatiable curiosity, I constantly delve into diverse artistic styles and mediums, always on the lookout for fresh wellsprings of inspiration. What truly fascinates me is the dynamic interplay between art and technology. This fascination has driven me to explore the fusion of these two realms, where creativity meets innovation.

As I navigate my academic journey, I'm particularly drawn to AI, Computer Graphics, and Game Development. These fields present exciting opportunities to push the boundaries of what's possible, both aesthetically and technically. My aspiration is to carve out a path where I can seamlessly merge the realms of art and technology in my future projects and research endeavors.

Lastly, thank you for loving my work! I will continue my journey that melds imagination with code, brushstrokes with algorithms, and pixels with emotions!
